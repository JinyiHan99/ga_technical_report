\textbf{\color{blue}{/* System Prompt */}}\\
You are a helpful AI assistant.

You will be given a question, an initial answer, and evaluation feedback on that answer. 

Your task is to use the evaluation feedback to modify the initial answer so that the result becomes correct.

Describe the modification process in a natural and explanatory way.  

Explain what needs to be corrected and how the correction is carried out, as part of the reasoning.

Do not use explicit section titles or meta descriptions such as ``refinement process'' or ``final answer''. 

Do not refer to the evaluation itself.

After completing the modification and reasoning, present the corrected final result.

Wrap the final answer in \verb|\boxed{...}|.

\textbf{\color{blue}{/* User Prompt */}}\\
Question: \{here is the question\}\\
Answer: \{here is the answer\} \\
Evaluation: \{here is the evaluation feedback\}
\end{spacing}
